Executive Summary

Game Concept

Explore a jungle world of mysteries with different types of characters you meet on the way, some are friendly while others try to hunt you down. Try to survive the hoard of creatures that you will face to complete your mission in this incredible adventure game. Complete levels and collect fruits you can get even more powerful with the power ups which are available which can be upgraded later on in game. In this adventure game, choose from 3 different characters to play from. Face unique bosses which have powerful attacks to destroy you so be careful while you face them as they provide an incredible and challenging experience.

Genre

Adventure, Thriller, Action

Target Audiance

For All Ages

Project Scope

The game will be a classic gameplay having different scenarios and places to pick from. The game will have simple yet beautiful graphics, easy and intuitive controls for any age user to easily be able to play it. An adventures environment brings the player closer to our character feelings and attaching more to the story and game play along the way. The game is suitable for all ages.

Story

One day a young boy from a very rich family went for a hunting trip and was attacked by a monster which separated him from his family which was taken hostage by the monster and left alone in the jungle. Now he has to survive the jungle life, to get back to his family. He needs your help. Run and jump through the deep jungle, avoid traps and clear all enemies in your way and defeat all the bosses and finally the monster to get back to his family.
